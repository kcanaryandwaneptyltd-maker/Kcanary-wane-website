Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What loan amounts do you offer? +

We offer loans starting from R500 up to a maximum of R10,000, depending on your needs and affordability.

What documents are required? +

Proof of occupation, proof of payment, proof of residence, and one witness (not living with you) with a valid South African ID.

How do repayments work? +

Choose from a 1-month one-time repayment (30% interest), a 3-month plan (22% per month), or a 6-month plan (17% per month).

How long does approval take? +

Our process is quick and hassle-free — most applications are reviewed and approved promptly once documents are submitted.

How do I contact the company? +

You can call or WhatsApp us on 078 883 1124, email us, or reach us via Facebook — see our Contact section below.
